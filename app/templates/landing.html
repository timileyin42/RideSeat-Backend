Features

Designed for Seamless Rides

Whether you are behind the wheel or booking a seat, Rideway provides robust tools for a smooth cost-sharing experience.

🚗

For Drivers

Offset your driving costs easily. Post your trip details, vehicle details, available seats, and price in just a few taps.

  • Post trips with customized pricing per seat
  • Register and manage multiple vehicles
  • Accept or reject passenger booking requests
  • Instant direct Stripe payouts after trip completion
📍

For Passengers

Find affordable travel routes across the UK. Search trips by city, compare driver ratings, and secure your booking instantly.

  • Search trips by origin, destination, and date
  • In-app direct messaging with your driver
  • Book individual or multiple seats securely
  • Escrow payments to protect your money

Find Your Perfect Ride
Seamless & Smart Search

Getting from point A to B has never been easier. Use our interactive map and list search to find a ride that matches your route, budget, and schedule.

1

Enter Your Route

Specify your start point and destination in the UK. Rideway immediately maps out the route and calculates potential travel corridors.

2

Compare Verified Drivers

View profiles of cost-sharing drivers heading along your route. Review prices per seat, available vehicle models, and driver ratings.

3

Book with Escrow

Reserve your seat with complete peace of mind. Payment is processed securely via Stripe and held in escrow until the trip is successfully completed.
